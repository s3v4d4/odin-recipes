Marinated pork recipe

Description

This is the recipe for nicely marinated grilled pork. The recipe is very straigthforward.

Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Make marinade: mix together all ingredients/li>
  2. Marinate the pork and place it in the refrigerator 24 hours
  3. Preheat the grill and oil it
  4. Remove the pork from the marinade and cook it on the grill for 20-30 min
