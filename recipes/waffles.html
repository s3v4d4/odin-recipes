Belgian Waffle recipe

Description

This is the recipe for homemade Belgian waffles. All you need are some simple ingredients and a waffle iron.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Baking powder
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Cooking spray
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat the waffle iron
  2. Break the eggs in a bowl
  3. Add milk, flower and vegetable oil and mix
  4. Add the remaining ingredients while mixing
  5. Put the batter on the waffle iron and close the top
  6. Remove the waffle after 3 to 5 min
